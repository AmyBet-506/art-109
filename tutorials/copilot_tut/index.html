What is Vitamin C?

Vitamin C, also known as ascorbic acid, is a powerful water-soluble vitamin that's essential for your body. It's like the superhero of vitamins – always there to save the day!

Amazing Benefits

  • 🚀 Boosts your immune system to fight off colds and flu
  • ✨ Acts as an antioxidant to protect your cells
  • 💪 Helps produce collagen for healthy skin, hair, and nails
  • ❤️ Supports heart health and reduces inflammation
  • 🧠 Enhances iron absorption for better energy

Where to Find Vitamin C

Load up on these delicious sources:

  • 🍊 Oranges and orange juice
  • 🍋 Lemons and limes
  • 🍓 Strawberries
  • 🫑 Bell peppers (especially red ones)
  • 🥦 Broccoli and Brussels sprouts
  • 🥝 Kiwi fruit

Fun Facts

Did you know? Humans are one of the few animals that can't produce their own vitamin C. That's why we need to get it from our diet – nature's way of saying "eat your fruits and veggies!"

Fun fact: Vitamin C was first isolated in 1928 and won the Nobel Prize in Chemistry in 1937. Talk about a star!
