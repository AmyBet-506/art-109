Hag's Bottle

Hag’s Bottle was modeled and partially sculpted in Blender. The cloth wrapped around the top of the bottle was done through Blender's cloth simulator. All the textures were painted in Adobe Substance Painter with the exception of the cloth texture which has an image texture base that was painted on top of. This bottle was inspired by classic Western witch iconography.
